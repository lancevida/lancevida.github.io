ArcherVerse

A Collection of Physically-Based Rendered 3D DLSU Scenes for Benchmarking Rendering Applications.

Developed by students from BS in Interactive Entertainment Major in Game Art & Design De La Salle University – College of Computer Studies.

The Team: Madison Franco, Jonathan Gonzalez, Lance Vida

Adviser: Dr. Neil Patrick Del Gallego Ph. D.

Overview

ArcherVerse is a series of photorealistic 3D scenes based on actual locations across De La Salle University’s Manila and Laguna campuses. Designed for both artistic visualization and rendering benchmark purposes, each scene is built using Physically-Based Rendering (PBR) and tested across modern engines. The scenes evaluates the realism, visual fidelity, and performance of rendering engines through detailed recreations of indoor and outdoor architectural spaces.
